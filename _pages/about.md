I am Chuanyang Jin, a junior undergraduate student in Honors Computer Science and Mathematics at New York University.

My research interests lie in machine learning, computer vision, and self-supervised learning. I am currently working with [Prof. Feng Chen](https://engineering.nyu.edu/faculty/chen-feng) on self-supervised visual recognition, and [Prof. Oded Regev](https://cims.nyu.edu/~regev/) on interpretable machine learning techniques to derive insights into biological processes. Prior to that, I spent time as a summer intern at Huawei and a teaching assistant at NYU.

Beyond my academic pursuit, I enjoy roaming in movies and books. I am an avid reader of physics, history, and scientific fiction, as well as a dedicated writer read by more than 15,000,000 times on different platforms. I also enjoy playing "Go", piano, and badminton.
