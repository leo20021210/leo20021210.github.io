I am Chuanyang Jin, a third-year undergrad in Honors Computer Science and Mathematics at New York University. 

My research interests lie in machine learning and computer vision. I am currently working with [Prof. Oded Regev](https://cims.nyu.edu/~regev/) on interpretable machine learning techniques to derive insights into biological processes, and [Prof. Feng Chen](https://engineering.nyu.edu/faculty/chen-feng) on self-supervised visual place recognition. Previously, I spent time as a summer intern at Huawei and a teaching assistant at NYU.

Beyond my current academic pursuit, I enjoy roaming in movies and books. I am an avid reader of physics, history, international relations, and scientific fiction, as well as a writer on different platforms with 30K followers. I also enjoy playing "Go", piano, and badminton.
