I am Peiqi Liu, an undergraduate student in Honors Computer Science and Mathematics at New York University.

My research interests lie in mobile manipulation and language conditional robotics. I am currently working with [Prof. Lerrel Pinto](https://www.lerrelpinto.com/) on long-horizon open vocabulary manipulation. Prior to that, I have worked with [Prof. Mengye Ren](https://mengyeren.com/) on self supervised computer vision representational learning during Spring 2023.

I am applying for P.h.D and MS programs Fall 2024. If you feel I am a good fit to your lab, feel free to contact me.
