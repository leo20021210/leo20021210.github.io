I am Peiqi Liu, an undergraduate student in Honors Computer Science and Mathematics at New York University.

My research interests lie in deep learning, computer vision, and self-supervised learning. I am currently working with [Prof. Lerrel Pinto]([https://www.lerrelpinto.com/]) on long-horizon open vocabulary manipulation. Prior to that, I have worked with [Prof. Mengye Ren]([https://mengyeren.com/]) on self supervised computer vision representational learning during Spring 2023.
